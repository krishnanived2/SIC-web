About Software Innovators Club

Software Innovators Club @ Amrita Kochi Campus aims to create a dynamic community

of developers, creators, and enthusiasts, providing them with opportunities for

learning, collaboration, and real-world problem-solving.


Membership to our club is open to all students of Amrita Kochi Campus with an interest

in developing something. The club provides an environment for students to extend their

ideas and interests and hence improve their overall technical skills.
